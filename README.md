# Noughts and Crosses

A classic tic-tac-toe game you can play in your browser. Challenge a friend or play against the computer!

Follow this link to access the live site:

https://sk-noughts-and-crosses.netlify.app/

### Getting Started

1. **Choose Your Game Mode**:

    - **Player vs Player**: Play with a friend on the same device
    - **Player vs Computer**: Challenge the AI opponent

2. **Select Your Symbol**:

    - Choose to play as **Noughts (O)** or **Crosses (X)**
    - In Player vs Player mode, Player 1 chooses first

3. **Make Your Move**:

    - Click on any empty square on the 3x3 grid
    - Players take turns placing their symbols

4. **Win the Game**:

    - Get three of your symbols in a row to win!
    - You can win horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
    - If all squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw

5. **Play Again**:
    - After a game ends, click "Ok" to start a new game
    - Use the back button to return to the main menu and change settings

-   The game is played on a 3x3 grid
-   Players alternate turns
-   The first player to get three of their symbols in a row wins
-   If all 9 squares are filled and no one has three in a row, it's a draw
-   You cannot place your symbol on a square that's already occupied
